Frequently Asked Questions

What is Disney+?

Disney+ is the home for Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic, Star and more.

What can I watch on Disney+?

Disney+ offers you a growing collection of stories:

  • Exclusive original series and movies
  • Timeless classics to recent hits from Walt Disney Animation Studios
  • Even more entertainment with comedies, dramas, true-story inspired content and so much more
  • Heartwarming stories for all ages animated by Pixar's talented artists
  • The entire Star Wars saga and much of the Marvel Cinematic Universe
  • National Geographic documentaries, plus special productions and much more
How much does Disney+ cost?

You can choose from three Disney+ subscription tiers:

  • Premium (€11.99/month or €119.90/year): Up to 4K Ultra HD and HDR resolution. 4 simultaneous streams. Downloads for offline viewing on up to 10 devices. Dolby Atmos sound on supported content. Ad-free.
  • Standard (€8.99/month or €89.90/year): Up to 1080p Full HD resolution. 2 simultaneous streams. Downloads for offline viewing on up to 10 devices. 5.1 surround sound on supported content. Ad-free.
  • Standard with ads (€5.99/month): Up to 1080p Full HD resolution. 2 simultaneous streams. 5.1 surround sound on supported content.

At the end of your subscription period, your subscription will automatically renew for the same period at the then-current Premium price (currently as indicated above), unless you cancel before or select another offer.

What devices and platforms can I use with Disney+?

Disney+ is accessible on mobiles, web browsers, game consoles, set-top boxes and smart TVs. Click here to see the complete list of compatible devices.

Does Disney+ subscription include a commitment period?

Disney+ is commitment-free and you can cancel your subscription anytime. Cancellation will take effect at the end of the period you've paid for. To unsubscribe, follow these 6 steps:

  1. Log in to www.DisneyPlus.com
  2. Select your profile
  3. Select Account
  4. Select your subscription
  5. Select Cancel Subscription
  6. Select Continue to cancel
